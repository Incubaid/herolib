

# ** Proposal for Strategic Advisory Collaboration **


# **Singularity Venture Hub - Threefold**

*September 2, 2025*

Dear Threefold Team,

At Singularity Venture Hub, we believe that transformative technologies need not only innovative design but also the right strategic, financial, and ecosystem support to truly thrive. Threefold embodies a bold vision for decentralized infrastructure, and we are excited about the opportunity to contribute to its next stage of growth.

Through our experience in tokenomics, fundraising, and network building, and by leveraging our global ecosystem of investors, launchpads, exchanges, and strategic partners, SVH is uniquely positioned to accelerate Threefold’s journey from concept to large-scale adoption.

This proposal outlines a performance-aligned collaboration model: we commit our expertise and network, while our success is tied directly to the capital raised and the strength of the ecosystem we help build together.

We see in Threefold not just a protocol, but a pioneering force in shaping the future of decentralized digital infrastructure. It would be our privilege to partner with you in ensuring that vision achieves its full potential.



1. **Value Proposition**

## 
    Through this collaboration, Threefold gains access to:

1. Proven expertise in token design and economic modelling
2. A global investor network spanning private investors, VCs, and institutions
3. A business development network covering exchanges, launchpads, market makers, and strategic partners
4. Ongoing strategic support to ensure the project’s fundraising success, sustainable growth, and ecosystem adoption
2. **Scope of Work**
1. **Tokenomics & Protocol Economy**
    1. Support with valuation, pricing, vesting schedules, and allocation market fit
    2. Advisory on protocol economy design to ensure long-term sustainability
    3. Development of monetization strategies, revenue streams, and sustainability mechanisms
    4. Advisory on potential applications and use cases that can be built on top of the protocol, with guidance on ecosystem design and expansion.
    5. Introduction to, and negotiation with, application builders and strategic partners to accelerate adoption and integration.
2. **Token Utility & Economic Design**
    6. Analysis of protocol success metrics and alignment with token incentives
    7. Mapping desired user behaviors and interaction models to the token economy
    8. Detailed tokenomics modelling and simulations to test economic assumptions
    9. Design of token utility and incentive systems, including loyalty/reward frameworks
3. **Fundraising & Operations**
    10. Sourcing, introduction, and management of private investors
    11. Sourcing, introduction, and management of venture capital investors
    12. Cap table management and advisory on allocation strategies for maximum strategic impact
4. **Go-To-Market Strategy**
    13. Development of a comprehensive GTM plan aligned with fundraising and token launch
    14. Community growth strategy (KOLs, influencers, grassroots campaigns)
    15. Marketing and narrative positioning for both retail and institutional audiences
    16. Launch strategy coordination across launchpads, exchanges, and partnerships
    17. Roadmap for sustainable user acquisition and retention beyond TGE
5. **Business Development & Network Expansion**
    18. Introduction and management of KOLs (Key Opinion Leaders) for marketing reach
    19. Introduction to Launchpads for successful retail distribution
    20. Introduction to Exchanges (CEX & DEX) to secure liquidity and accessibility
    21. Introduction to Market Makers for trading efficiency and liquidity depth
    22. Introduction to Security Audit Firms for technical due diligence and credibility
    23. Introduction to potential partners across the blockchain and Web3 ecosystem
3. **Commercial Terms**

    The commercial framework is designed to align incentives, ensure transparency, and maximize the success of the collaboration. 

1. **Token Supply Allocation**
    1. Guaranteed Allocation: 2% of total token supply (an additional 0.5% will be allocated to SVH for every incremental USD 500,000 raised beyond the first USD 1,000,000).
    2. Cap: The total allocation under this mechanism will not exceed 4% of the total token supply.
1. **Monthly Retainer**
    3. Commencement: Payable upon the successful completion of the first USD 1,000,000 total raised by the Threefold/SVH.
    4. Amount: USD 20,000 per month.
    5. Scope of Access: Grants the project all the points covered in the SoW, full access to the Singularity Venture Hub incubation team (7 people) and also includes the possibility for the Singularity Venture Hub team to formally join as Token Team, an element that can be highlighted in project decks, investor materials, and strategic communications.
2. **Fundraising & Sales Success Fees**
    6. Fundraising/Sales: 10% of all capital raised through SVH introductions and investor networks and/or product sales directly attributable to SVH’s business development efforts.
